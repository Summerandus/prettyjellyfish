pretty

001 — about

who
am I?

A creature of thresholds — living between languages, between hemispheres, between the world of data and the world of feeling. I make sense of systems so others don't have to hold the whole map alone.

When I'm not untangling data, I'm in the water. Swimming teaches me what stillness actually costs, and what it gives back.

“The jellyfish does not try to be translucent.
It simply is.”
